# About This Project

Kerpen, like many towns in Germany, faces challenges from changing rainfall patterns and the need for climate-adaptive water management in residential areas.

**Goal:**  
This website helps homeowners in Kerpen discover sustainable, affordable solutions for rainwater management at home.

**What you'll find here:**
- Practical recommendations for rainwater reuse, infiltration, and flood protection
- Maps showing which solutions fit different housing types and soils
- Easy steps for implementing solutions on your own property

Designed for everyone, this guide is part of a research project aiming to make eco-friendly rainwater management simple and accessible for private homeowners.
