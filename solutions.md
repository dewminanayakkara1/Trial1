# Explore Rainwater Management Solutions

Discover which options match your home type and local climate conditions.

## Green Solutions

- **Rain Gardens**  
  Shallow planted areas that absorb rainwater and reduce flooding risks.
  - *Best for gardens and yards with permeable soils.*

- **Green Roofs**  
  Roofs covered with plants help slow rainwater runoff and keep homes cool.
  - *Useful for multi-family buildings and garages.*

## Engineered Solutions

- **Rainwater Storage Tanks**  
  Store rainwater for garden irrigation or toilet flushing.
  - *Simple installation for single homes and row houses.*

- **Permeable Pavements**  
  Special paving materials that allow rainwater to soak into the ground.
  - *Great for driveways and patios, reduces surface runoff.*

## Hybrid Solutions

- **Infiltration & Reuse Systems**  
  Combine tanks and underground soakaways for maximum benefit.
  - *Ideal for homes with both hard surfaces and open space.*
